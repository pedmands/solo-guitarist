Biography

Preston Clark Edmands is a songwriter and musician from Maine, USA. With no formal training in music, he learned own his own to play, sing, and compose music. Today, he works as a lounge entertainer, performing artist, and audio engineer, while furthering his education independently.

Preston loves working and playing with other musicians from all around the world, and gets a fair bit of travel in, himself.

His work has taken him to over twenty countries, including Thailand, Malta, Italy, and Jamaica.

But Preston’s roots will always be intwined in New England and her various charms, having cast their spell upon him in early childhood. “There’s a certain stoicism that comes with winter in Maine, and that lends itself well to the artistically inclined. We have the need to create, no matter what the external circumstances may be,” Edmands opines.
